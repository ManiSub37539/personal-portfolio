Raspberry-Pi Personal Offline Voice Assistant

A Raspberry-Pi & Local LLM powered voice assistant which accepts vocal inputs and delivers verbal feedback. However, this voice assistant is meant to be more than just a chat-buddy. It is meant to greatly improve the productivity of some of the laziest, most non-productive people in the world (such as myself). Instead of spending precious time and energy moving my fingers across a keyboard, I aim to simply execute tasks through giving vocal instructions to Money - be it from simply bouncing ideas back and forth, to drafting and sending complete emails, executing SSH comands across multiple devices, and managing my homelab - all by voice.

My Personal Homelab

Here's how I'm running & actively expanding a homelab which hosts a network-wide ad blocker, a self-hosted media server which hosts enough music, movies and shows to cancel both my Netflix & Spotify subscriptions, a Kali virtual machine which serves as a Pen Testing Environment, and a Windows VM which serves as a Active Directory Sandbox - all on my 4 year old Huawei Matebook laptop with 16 gigabytes of RAM.
